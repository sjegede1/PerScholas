Daft Punk was a groundbreaking French electronic music duo formed in 1993 by Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo. They became known for their distinctive robotic personas and their fusion of house, disco, and funk elements in their music. With iconic tracks like "Around the World" and "Get Lucky," Daft Punk achieved global success and influenced countless artists in the electronic music scene. After an illustrious career spanning nearly three decades, the duo announced their split in 2021, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and timeless music.
